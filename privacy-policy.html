Privacy Policy for TINKIT Powerstation Monitor

Effective Date: July 3, 2026

TINKIT Powerstation Monitor is developed by TINKIT IT Solutions. This app is designed to help users monitor compatible TINKIT Powerstation and JKBMS Bluetooth-based battery systems.

Information We Collect

TINKIT Powerstation Monitor does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, contacts, messages, photos, or location data.

Bluetooth and Device Permissions

The app may request Bluetooth or nearby device permissions in order to scan for and connect to compatible JKBMS Bluetooth devices. These permissions are used only for the app’s powerstation and battery monitoring function.

Battery Monitoring Data

When connected to a compatible device, the app may display battery-related information such as voltage, current, battery status, temperature, and other battery management system readings. These readings are used only for monitoring purposes inside the app.

Data Sharing

TINKIT Powerstation Monitor does not sell, trade, or share user personal data with third parties.

Data Storage

The app does not intentionally store personal user data on external servers. Battery monitoring data, if displayed, is used for app functionality and monitoring purposes.

Third-Party Services

The app may use standard Android and Google Play services required for app distribution, security, and compatibility.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children. It is intended for users who own or operate compatible powerstation or battery monitoring equipment.

Data Deletion

Since the app does not require account registration and does not collect personal user accounts, there is no account data to delete. For privacy-related concerns, users may contact TINKIT IT Solutions.
